Why choose Amansala?

Everything you need for an unforgettable retreat

Amazing Location

Tulum is rated as one of the top 5 beaches in the world and is the perfect destination to host a retreat, offering miles of white sandy beaches and turquoise blue water.

Beautiful Setting

It is a village-like setting with five gorgeous yoga shalas, vast grounds, with lounge areas, a beachfront dinning deck and a variety of rooms.

We Make It Easy

Our staff can assist you to curate the perfect experience from start to finish. We’re experts at executing successful retreats, allowing you to do what you love and we help with all the rest.

Spa and Soul Services

Unforgettable pampering with our talented team of therapist. Add on our signature Mayan Clay ritual, a Temazcal sweat lodge, ice bath or cacao and sound healing.

Add on Adventures

Ad some adventure to your retreat by taking a tour of Mayan ruins, swimming in cenotes, kayaking through the mangroves or floating in cristal blue water

Delicious Healthy Food

Fresh, delicious food that can be tailored to meet your needs. Our motto is from the earth to your plate.
