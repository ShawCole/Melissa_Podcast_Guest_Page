Beach Houses in Tulum

Our beach houses in Tulum, Mexico are great for a group of friends or families.

On-Site Villa

Casa Grande

It is a spacious Tulum-style house with polished cement floors and walls. You have the best of both worlds, your own private house yet with all the hotel's services.

Upstairs: shared lounge area; master bedroom with king bed and small terrace; 2 small bedrooms with queen beds sharing bathroom; den with twin bed.

Downstairs: large living room; kitchen with fridge and patio; 1 bedroom with king bed and private bathroom; 1 bedroom with two queen beds and private bathroom.

Access to Amansala beach club included.

Keep in mind that this house is on the grounds of a hotel. We host fitness and yoga retreats, so we have classes near the house with music. The classes do not start before 10 a.m.; please take note that you may hear music during the day after 10:30 a.m. and again at 4:30 p.m.

6-Bedroom House

Casa Shanti

Casa Shanti is a beautiful, modern 6-bedroom house in the middle of the resort, perfect for families, bachelorettes, or a trip with friends. When you step inside, you are in your world.

  • 3 rooms with king bed
  • 3 rooms with 2 queen beds

Each room has its own private bathroom and ample closet space, finished with smooth concrete and local accents.

Common spaces: living room with sofa; small kitchen with fridge, burners for cooking. The kitchen is not set up as a chef's master kitchen.

This house is located on the hotel grounds where we host fitness and yoga retreats. The classes do not start before 10 a.m.; please take note that you may hear music during the day after 10:30 a.m. and again at 4:30 p.m. This house is not suitable for anyone looking to host parties with loud music as it is on the hotel grounds.

Boutique Hotel

Mojave Boutique Hotel

Great neighborhood, the best zone in Tulum! Friday nights are a bit congested as there are parties nearby with music at Gitano, so if you want to be in a more peaceful setting, that would not be a good choice!

  • 9 rooms with king bed, each with private bathroom
  • 1 room with two queen beds and private bathroom

They are all near each other and share a beautiful rooftop deck.

Amenities: shops, cafe, general lounging area; cute cafe and bar downstairs for breakfast, coffee, drinks, late evening snacks.

In the happening zone of Tulum, a short walk from Gitano and Rosa Negra.

If you would like to visit the Amansala beach club, you can get a discount of 25 USD entry plus consumption.

Private Beachfront

Akumal Beachfront House

This private modern hideaway is a minimalistic five-bedroom beachfront house in a great location that is within reach of Tulum yet outside of the crowds and traffic.

Tucked away in a quiet neighborhood outside of Tulum, Akumal Sur, it gives you easy access to explore the Riviera Maya in a secluded private setting. Directly in front is a gorgeous view, but to swim, you must walk 5 minutes to the right and be on a sandy beach. The house has a private pool.

Upstairs: Room with two twin beds; room with queen bed with beautiful view; room with queen bed.

Downstairs: Master bedroom with stunning view terrace and king bed with private bathroom; 1 room with king bed.

Common spaces: Fully equipped kitchen; double-height dining room; gorgeous deck and pool.

If you want to visit the Amansala Beach Club, there is a discount for just 25 USD entry plus consumption.
