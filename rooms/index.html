Our Rooms

A haven where natural beauty meets style

Made with Love by Local Mayan Craftsmen.

Each room embodies the essence of Tulum

Each room embodies the essence of Tulum using smooth cement, Mayan stone, and centuries-old wooden columns; multiple staircases weave together the spaces, giving the feel of a modern-day Swiss Family Robinson.

Organic cotton sheets, comfortable beds, a farm-to-table kitchen, Mayan healing massages, attention to detail, and personalized customer service. Join us for a stay where you can do your own thing, or Join us for a curated retreat blending yoga, fitness, massage, and mindfulness.
