The perfect blend of authenticity and comfort

What started as a 4-room pop-up has become one of the most iconic beachfront resorts in Tulum, Mexico.

Renowned for its wabi-sabi design using organic materials, we provide guests with an authentic, rejuvenating experience at our Tulum eco-resort.

Whether you join one of our in-house yoga or wellness retreats or book a room, you will find community and a place you want to revisit again and again.

It is recognized for its eco-chic accommodations, yoga retreat, spa, massage and fitness classes.

It is a perfect blend of authenticity and comfort.

Amansala is an all-inclusive resort in Tulum, Mexico, and the city’s first resort.

Melissa Perlman followed her instinct and a deep passion for creating a holiday with a purpose — one that sends you home looking and feeling better than when you arrived.

Amansala is located on Tulum’s beach road (Boca Paila) at KM 5.5, along a beautiful stretch of Caribbean coastline between the Tulum Ruins and the Sian Ka’an Biosphere Reserve.
