Her Story

She Followed Her Instinct

What started as a simple idea just after 9/11 while sitting in a studio apartment in NYC became one of the most recognized wellness destinations in the world.

“You could say we were ahead of the curve. Maybe you’d call it instinct. I just knew this was the place.”

Long before wellness was an industry. Long before Tulum was on anyone’s radar. She built Amansala and created Bikini Bootcamp from scratch. In a predominantly male-dominated industry, Melissa created a niche that incorporates style and comfort with purpose and community.

Twenty-five years later, the town has changed beyond recognition. But Amansala is still here. Still rooted. Still real.
