Questions & Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

Does Amansala have enough rooms for a large wedding?
Amansala is made up of two beachfront hotels just seconds apart, with a combined capacity of up to 150 guests. We offer discounted room blocks for weddings booking four nights or more. For a more intimate experience, you can opt for a full buyout of our Chica property, which includes 25 rooms and 43 beds.
What if there is bad weather?
If there is bad weather, we have many options at Amansala, including two indoor spaces that can accommodate large groups and be transformed into beautiful party and dining venues.
Do I need a wedding planner in Tulum, Mexico?
We can also arrange a call with our onsite coordinator to review all the options with you. We work with trusted wedding planners who can be dedicated to your event, with packages starting from $900–$1,500 USD, depending on your needs.
Can we request a certain menu?
Yes, you can provide us with the wedding menu of your choice, and we will do our best to accommodate our excellent chefs.
What if you want to extend the party to longer than the allocated time?
The party would have to end by 11 but you could extend up to 1:00 am for a fee of 300 USD per hour.
What about gratuity?
15% will be added to your event, and if you would like to leave extra the night of the event, the staff would appreciate it.
What are the payment terms?
A $2,500 deposit is required to reserve your dates. For cancellations made more than 60 days in advance, $1,250 will be refunded. Cancellations within 60 days are non-refundable unless due to a natural disaster, in which case we will reschedule. We’re happy to support unique, customized visions with a coordinator. Site visits are available with up to 50% discounted stay, depending on the time of year and availability.
