Biography

Melissa Perlman

Melissa Perlman is a dynamic entrepreneur, visionary hotelier, and wellness pioneer whose trailblazing spirit has helped shape Tulum into the wellness destination it is today. After relocating to Tulum following 9/11, Melissa began building Amansala, a beachfront health and wellness retreat, literally room by room.

Her passion, grit, and creativity transformed that vision into one of the largest and most iconic properties in Tulum, now boasting over 100 rooms, multiple yoga shalas, beachfront restaurants, and a thriving wellness community.

Melissa is also the founder of Bikini Bootcamp, an innovative and wildly popular fitness retreat that combines movement, mindfulness, and adventure. Launched long before wellness travel became mainstream, Bikini Bootcamp helped redefine what it means to reset—blending results-driven workouts with the soul of a beachside escape.

In 2017, Melissa expanded her entrepreneurial journey by becoming a partner in Gitano Tulum and Grupo Gitano. As an active investor and strategic partner, she plays a key role in guiding the business alongside founder James Gardner, bringing her strong business acumen and deep understanding of brand building, hospitality, and experiential travel.

Widely recognized as a thought leader in the global wellness and boutique hotel industry, Melissa is known for her unique ability to create spaces that feel both inspiring and deeply rooted in nature. Her influence spans curated wellness experiences and luxury lifestyle concepts that blend authenticity with elegance.
