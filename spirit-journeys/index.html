Mayan Clay

A ceremony focusing on letting go of the old, to make room for the new. The mineral rich locally sourced Mayan clay starts with an explanation, followed by a self application, then a beachside meditation, concluding with a swim in the beautiful Caribbean sea. Leaving you feeling deeply connected to yourself and ready to move forward with love and certainty.

Amansala Resort & Spa offers authentic Tulum spiritual experiences, blending ancient traditions with modern wellness. Each sacred journey is designed to restore balance, heal the body and mind, and connect you deeply with nature. Whether you are seeking a spiritual retreat in Tulum or a revitalizing yoga escape, Amansala provides a truly transformative experience.
