Cenote Tours, Mangrove Kayaking & Mayan Ruins in Tulum

Tulum, Mexico, is an enchanting destination that blends history, nature, and adventure. Whether you're looking for a Tulum ruins tour, an exciting cenote excursion in Tulum, or a peaceful mangrove adventure, this vibrant region offers unforgettable experiences. From exploring ancient Mayan sites to swimming in hidden jungle cenotes, Tulum is the perfect place for travelers seeking both relaxation and excitement.

Tour

Tulum Ruins

Perched above the turquoise waters of the Riviera Maya, the Tulum ruins are a must-visit. Our guided Tulum ruins tour takes you through this open-air museum, where a knowledgeable local guide shares fascinating insights into Mayan history. The tour includes transportation, entrance fees, and lasts approximately 2.5 hours.

Tour

Muyil Tour

Float through ancient Mayan canals in the Sian Ka'an Biosphere Reserve on the Muyil Float Tour, one of the most serene experiences near Tulum. A perfect balance of nature, relaxation, and culture for wellness travelers staying at Amansala.

Tour

Private Mangrove

Explore the heart of the jungle, we'll take you to a beautiful, hidden swimming hole where you can kayak, take a dip, or just relax and enjoy the setting. Don't forget to take your swimwear! Including transportation and a guide, lasts approximately 2.5 hours.

Activity

Jungle Gym Tour

Train outdoors at Tulum Jungle Gym, a beachfront open-air fitness space known for its wooden equipment and ocean views. A popular activity for active and wellness-focused travelers visiting Tulum.

Tour

Grande Cenote

Grand Cenote, is about 15 minutes drive from Amansala. Explore the world underwater with a set of snorkels and lots of beautiful things to see. If you are lucky, you will swim side by side with a little turtle. Includes transportation, snorkel gear, entrance fees and a guide. Approx. 2.5 hours

Activity

Padel Tulum

Padel is a popular sport in Tulum, with jungle courts just minutes from the beach. Amansala is located only 7 minutes from Jungle Padel, making it an ideal base for guests planning an active, padel-focused stay.
