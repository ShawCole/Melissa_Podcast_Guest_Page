7 Gotas Villa

Private Oceanfront Villa in Akumal Sur Near Tulum, Mexico

Modern 5-Bedroom Villa with Pool, Ocean Views & Beach Access

This private modern hideaway is in a great location within Tulum’s reach yet outside of the crowds and traffic. Tucked away in a quiet neighborhood, Akumal Sur, it gives you easy access to explore the Riviera Maya in a secluded private setting. Directly in front is a gorgeous view, but to swim, you must walk 5 minutes to the right and be on a sandy beach. The house has a private pool.
